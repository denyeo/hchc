Reconnect with old friends and the Hwa Chong spirit
at the inaugural Homecoming for the Hwa Chong family of schools.

That shared uniform made us family. Now our family is making waves in a range of fields and professions. Come listen to fellow alumni share about their journeys and how their time in Hwa Chong shaped them, at the first ever Hwa Chong Alumni Homecoming event. It's a time for the entire HC Family - students, staff & alumni of all HC schools - to come together, catch up, and celebrate the HC Spirit.

This August, the Hwa Chong Alumni Association is proud to present to you Homecoming 2015, where we can gather once again as a family. We'll talk, we'll laugh, we'll eat together. Just like old times.

Talks by Distinguished Alumni

Hear and be inspired by alumni who have achieved success in a range of fields. We are proud to have them return for this occasion to share their knowledge and experiences!

Networking and Interest Groups

Meet old schoolmates and other alumni based on Career, Entrepreneurship & Parenting interest groups. We've invited alumni in various fields to give short talks about their interests and businesses, to get the conversations started!

Foodfest/Bazaar, Tours & Funfair

Hwachongians are big in business and F&B. Enjoy delicacies from stalls set up by alumni-linked businesses such as the Tung Lok Group, find out what other businesses alumni are running, take a shot at funfair games, and go on a nostalgic campus tour.
